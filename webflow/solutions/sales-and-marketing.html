How clients can schedule time to meet with you or your sales team

1. Share your availability

Customers ca easily schedule time to chat with you by checking your availability in real-time (individual or team availability).

2. Customers will select dates and times to chat

FreeBusy will warn them if you or members of your sales team are free or busy at that time.

You or your sales team will receive an email with a link to view the meeting on your calendars, with all the meeting details. You can update the meeting details anytime.
