How many meetings do you attend every month? Most teachers and coaches have more than just one calendar to juggle. For many it’s the private work calendar, the staff calendar, the monthly meeting/PTA calendar, the student/office hours’ calendar and the personal calendar.

Keeping track of appointments across them all are one thing but what about the last-minute reschedules, the “no-shows” or the scheduling conflicts and duplicate events?

Before now you needed to be a calendar master and not only keep track of all these moving pieces but if you ever need to schedule with more than one person you must also spend the hours every week chasing colleagues or students down to for “best” times to meet.

If you are trying to schedule with a group, this usually means not only playing coordinator for everyone’s availability but selecting the exact time slot that is free for everyone.

How much time do you spend per week on any of the above?

Imagine there was a way to send out only one invite and to be able to get the best time from everybody—with zero back and forth? Now you can with FreeBusy.

FreeBusy is an online scheduling assistant for teachers and coaches that allows the following:
